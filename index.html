UNIVERSITY OF IBADAN DEPARTMENT OF MUSIC

(A citadel of musical knowledge and culture)

Department of Music one of the first department in the faculty of arts. it was founded in 1977. The department is well known through the nation for the seasoned and well-equipped musicians and graduate it produces. it cuts across all aspects and genres of music all through the world. The following genres can be learnt:

  • Jazz
  • Gospel
  • Blues
  • Rock and Roll
  • Pop
  • R&B
